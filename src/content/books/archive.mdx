  <p>I created this book for my MFA thesis. It's a worldbuilding project I've been ruminating on for a few years. I created the characters, creatures, and settings to explore themes of societal collapse and renewal.</p>
  <p>What are the limits of human collaboration? Will our fractiousness and individualism as a species be our downfall? Could they also contain the key to our survival? I aim to explore these questions through the fantasy game world of ArcHive.</p>
