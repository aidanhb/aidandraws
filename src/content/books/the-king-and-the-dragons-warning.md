This is a bittersweet story in the vein of old school children’s fables. I love old tragedies and cautionary tales, so I decided to make on of my own regarding an impending disaster.

The goal was to evoke an ancient, wood cut aesthetic, inspired by games I grew up on. I was able to create the desired aesthetic fully digitally using Procreate brushes and textures. I also made a custom font for it!
